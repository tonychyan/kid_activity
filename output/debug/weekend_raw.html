Top Weekend Picks for Austin Families: June 6-8

Big weekend energy incoming! Between Juneteenth celebrations, a community-wide family reunion, a rainbow of Pride fun, and even a shot at an ice cream-throwing world record (were not making this up), theres no shortage of ways to get out and play.

Oh — and dont forget! Fathers Day is coming up on Sunday, June 16. Get a head start on planning something awesome for dad with our top picks right here:How to Treat Dad to the Best Fathers Day Ever

Enjoy your weekend, Austin!

*Top photo credit: UMLAUF Sculpture Garden and Museum

— Mufasa: The Lion King @ Community First! Village

Friday, June 6 | 8 pm | RSVP for FREE here

Enjoy a fun, family-friendly movie night under the stars at Community First! Village! Bring your lawn chairs, blankets, and favorite people to this unique outdoor cinema experience. Concessions like popcorn, candy, and drinks will be available for purchase (cards only), and all sales directly support formerly homeless neighbors working the event. Youre also invited to take a guided tour of the Village before the movie begins. Admission is free, leashed pups are welcome, and youll want to dress for the weather and pack a flashlight and bug spray. Movies begin at full dark!

— Juneteenth @ Mueller

Saturday, June 7 | 11 am – 3:30 pm | FREE

Celebrate Juneteenth with music, art, and big community energy at Mueller! This free, family-friendly event features DJs, live art, food trucks, a Kids Zone, vendors, door prizes, guest speakers, and more, all in honor of culture, freedom, and joy. Swing by 2006 Philomena St. on June 7 from 11 am – 3:30 pm and bring the whole crew for a day of fun, learning, and celebration.

— Freedom & Juneteenth @ Pease Park

Saturday, June 7 | 6 pm – 10 pm | FREE with Registration here

Pease Park is hosting a special Juneteenth celebration with a Family Reunion vibe — think music, games, great food, and joyful connection. Bring your lawn chairs and blankets for a full evening of fun featuring UNO, Spades, and dominoes, plus food for purchase from Big Mayne BBQ and Southern Soul Bistro. Enjoy a DJ set, sip & paint (first come, first served), a community talk on the food of Juneteenth, and live jazz from The Keezy Experience. The event is free, but registration is encouraged! Register here.

— Amy’s Fest @ Bouldin Acres

Sunday, June 8 | 11 am – 3 pm | FREE

Amys Fest is back and bringing the sprinkles! Head to this free, all-ages celebration at Bouldin Acres this Sunday for a day packed with sweet treats, local vendors, games, a live DJ, and even a dog costume contest. Plus, dont miss your chance to be part of anIce Cream Throwing World Record(yes, really!). Its all in support of Emancipet and their incredible work helping pets and their people. Come for the fun, stay for the feel-good vibes!

— Family Day: PRIDE! @ UMLAUF Sculpture Garden & Museum

Sunday, June 8 | 11 am – 4 pm | Register for FREE here

Celebrate Pride Month with UMLAUF Family Day: PRIDE, a free event designed to uplift Austins LGBTQIA+ youth and bring the whole community together through art, creativity, and connection. Enjoy hands-on crafts, sculpture scavenger hunts, a UT Kids Lab pop-up, and community partners like ChrysalisQ and Equality Texas. Dont miss storytime with Meredith Wilson at 12 pm, 1 pm, and 2 pm, each reading includes student ASL interpreters. Its a joyful day of learning, expression, and celebration for all ages! Get more info here.

More to explore this weekend:

  • Artisan Mercado: 2025 Edition @ 506 Congress Ave.
  • Music Under the Star: Luke Bulla with Sentimental Family Band @ Bullock Museum
  • Groundwork Music Project @ Cherrywood Coffee House
  • Dont Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus: The Musical @ Zach
  • Dinos in the Wild @ Ladybird Johnson Wildflower Center
